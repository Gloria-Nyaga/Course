MY FIRST WEEK AT AKIRACHIX

Hello there I am Gloria Nyaga and I want to tell you more about my first week at Akirachix.

Akirachix has been an amazing experience so far. I love the environment since it is beautiful, peaceful, clean and really accomodative.
The meals are delicious, the sleeping areas are really comfortable and the students are friendly and ready to help where needed. I love
how we are a diverse community and despite that no one treats the other differently. Moreover, it consists of an amazing team that run
the program from the staff that maintain the school, to the alumnus, teachng assistants, educators and the administators who are ready
to guide and help us whenever needed.

GOOD EXPERIENCES

  1. The places to sleep are comfortable and clean.
  2. The food is healthy and delicious.
  3. The educators are teaching well and the teaching assistants are helpful when stack.
  4. The students though diverse are friendly and ready to help each other when stuck in class.
  5. I love learning new things, and so far I am learning new things

BAD EXPERIENCES

  • The place is super cold and it took some time for me to adjust.
  • Learning new things for a short period of time can be overwhelming.
  • Sometimes there is a shortage of water in the bathrooms or electrical failures.
  • Adapting to a new environment while interacting with new people can be overwhelming.
